A Home for Hearts & Paws

At Purrfect Brews, we combine the comfort of exceptional coffee with the joy of feline companionship. Our resident cats are all rescues looking for their forever homes, and every visit helps support local animal welfare while giving you the therapeutic benefit of cat therapy.

For the safety and comfort of everyone, humans and animals alike, we have put in place some simple rules to follow when visiting Purrfect Brews:

  • Children under 12 must be supervised
